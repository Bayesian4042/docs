---
title: "What is Gema"
description: "A multi-tenant clinic patient support platform powered by AI companions"
---

Gema is a **multi-tenant clinic patient support platform**. Clinics onboard onto the platform, configure AI-powered companions (chatbots), and assign them to patients. Patients interact with the AI via the **Tesigo mobile app**. Clinic staff manage everything through a **web dashboard**.

<Warning>
  The AI strictly retrieves information from curated plans and knowledge bases — it does **not** give medical advice.
</Warning>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clinic onboards">
    A clinic joins the platform, configures its branding, AI settings, and notification preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff build companions">
    Clinic staff create AI companions — each with a system prompt, linked knowledge bases, treatment plans, follow-up schedules, and intent shortcuts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Companion assigned to patient">
    A companion is assigned to a specific patient. At assignment time, schedules and plan links are snapshotted so later edits don't affect the active companion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Patient interacts via Tesigo">
    The patient uses the Tesigo mobile app to chat with the AI. The AI retrieves relevant content and collects symptom check-ins — no free-form medical advice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff review and grade">
    Clinic staff monitor conversations, grade them (BLUE / YELLOW / RED), and send direct messages when needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key concepts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Companion (Behavior)" icon="robot">
    The core unit — an AI configuration tied to a single patient. Includes system prompt, knowledge base links, plan links, follow-up schedules, and intent shortcuts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Base" icon="book">
    Clinic-curated articles chunked and embedded for RAG retrieval. The AI answers only from this content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Treatment Plan" icon="file-medical">
    Structured patient plans with embedded content. Linked to companions and optionally bundled with Shopify product packages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conversation" icon="comments">
    A thread between a patient and their companion (plus direct staff messages). Graded and monitored by clinic staff.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
